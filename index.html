    How this is calculated
    Your rebooking incentive = rebookings × your per-rebooking rate. The rate rises with your average rating (last 50 jobs):
    • 4.85–4.89★ → 8 SAR per rebooking
    • 4.90–4.96★ → 15 SAR per rebooking
    • 4.97★ and above → 18 SAR per rebooking
    Below 4.85★ there's no rebooking incentive, and it starts once you're 30 days past your approval date. Full payslips also include commission (3% of monthly GMV above SAR 15,000 when rated 4.85★+), 100% of tips, and referral and shadowing bonuses.
