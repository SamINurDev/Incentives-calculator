How to use this page

  1. Type your L-number in the box above and tap Find my earnings.
  2. Your activity fills in automatically — your total and a full breakdown appear on the right.
  3. Drag the sliders for jobs, rating and rebookings to see how your earnings change.
  4. The Maximum potential box shows what you could reach, and how to get there.

  1. Enter your L-number and tap Find my earnings to load your figures.
  2. Read your estimated incentives and the coloured breakdown of where they come from.
  3. Move the sliders to test "what if" — more jobs, a higher rating, more rebookings.
  4. Open Other incentives to add referrals, shares, shadowing or new-pro ratings.
  5. Use the Maximum potential figure as your target for the month.
  • Rebooking — rebooked jobs × your rating tier: SAR 18 at 4.97★+, 15 at 4.90–4.96, 8 at 4.85–4.89, none below 4.85. Requires working more than 30 days.
  • Commission — 3% of monthly GMV above SAR 15,000 (GMV = jobs × avg service value). None if your rating is below 4.85.
  • New pro — SAR 5 per 5-star rating minus SAR 3 per rating of 3.5 or below, for your first 150 jobs only. Never below zero.
  • Share profile — SAR 50 each time a customer's share link converts to a delivered service within 7 days (rating 4.85+).
  • Referral — SAR 500 per candidate you refer who passes interview and arrives in KSA. Tips — 100% of tips, always.
  • Shadowing — SAR 50 if your assigned mentee averages above 4.8 across their first 10 jobs.

Figures are indicative and exclude deductions. Rating tiers use your average over the last 50 rated jobs. Commission uses the 3%-above-SAR-15,000 rule on GMV after discounts.
