  2. Add your tips, base salary, and anything under Other incentives.

  • Rebooking — rebooked jobs × your rating tier: SAR 18 at 4.97★+, 15 at 4.90–4.96, 8 at 4.85–4.89, none below 4.85. Requires working more than 30 days.
  • Commission — 3% of monthly GMV above SAR 15,000 (GMV = jobs × avg service value). None if your rating is below 4.85.
  • New pro — SAR 5 per 5-star rating minus SAR 3 per rating of 3.5 or below, for your first 150 jobs only. Never below zero.
  • Share profile — SAR 50 each time a customer's share link converts to a delivered service within 7 days (rating 4.85+).
  • Referral — SAR 500 per candidate you refer who passes interview and arrives in KSA. Tips — 100% of tips, always.
  • Shadowing — SAR 50 if your assigned mentee averages above 4.8 across their first 10 jobs.

Figures are indicative and exclude deductions. This page stores no personal data — any figures shown come only from your own private link or what you type in.
